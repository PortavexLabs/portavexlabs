Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: July 1, 2025

Portavex Labs Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all Portavex mobile applications — Portavex: Analyze, Portavex: Compare, Portavex: Screen, and Portavex: Educate — and to this website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our Apps:

  • Information you provide directly: Your name (optional, for personalization only), portfolio data you enter manually, and screening criteria you set.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the Apps, in aggregate and not linked to your identity.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and app version, used for compatibility and bug fixing.
  • Purchase information: Subscription status managed through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store or process your payment card details.
  • AI query content: When you use AIDA, your queries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API via a secure server-side proxy. We do not store your query history beyond the duration of your session.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your brokerage account credentials or connect to your brokerage.
  • We do not collect your Social Insurance Number, Social Security Number, or government-issued ID.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party advertisers.
  • Our Apps are ad-free. We do not display advertising.
  • We do not collect your precise location.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Apps
  • Process subscription purchases and verify entitlements
  • Personalize your in-app experience
  • Diagnose technical issues and crashes
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — subscription billing and purchase validation.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and entitlement verification.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI-powered query interpretation via AIDA. Queries are routed through our secure server-side proxy.
  • Supabase — backend database and server functions, hosted on secure cloud infrastructure.

5. Data Storage and Security

Portfolio data you enter is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. It is not transmitted to our servers unless you use a feature that requires it (such as AI analysis). All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

6. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its data. Server-side session data used for AI queries is not retained after your session ends.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below.

9. Canadian Privacy Rights (PIPEDA)

Portavex Labs Inc. is incorporated in Canada and complies with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to access your personal information and to challenge its accuracy.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of our Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
